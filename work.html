GOQii

GOQii - Video Production

One of the best ways to ensure your fitness goals are fulfilled is to use a fitness tracker that monitors your daily activities. Goqii helps you in knowing how much you have achieved in your fitness training project which boosts your motivation levels. These trackers assist in keeping a record of your exercise statistics, generate infographics and reports.
We collaborated with fitness and fashion Youtuber BeerBiceps, lifestyle vlogger Mumbiker Nikhil and travel and lifestyle blogger Larissa Dsa who were the faces of the campaign. These influencers created brand awareness by advising the audience to adopt a healthy lifestyle using the hashtag #TakeTheFirstStep by keeping a track of their daily activities with the Goqii fitness tracker. We also encouraged the audience to share their fitness journey using the #TakeTheFirstStep.
